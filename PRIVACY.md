# Privacy Policy for Vani: Indic TTS

**Last Updated:** February 8, 2026

## Data Collection & Usage

Vani: Indic TTS is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension:

### What We DO:
- ✅ Store your Sarvam AI API key locally in your browser using `chrome.storage.local`
- ✅ Store your language, voice, and speed preferences locally
- ✅ Send selected text to Sarvam AI API for text-to-speech conversion
- ✅ Access page content when you click "Play" (only when requested)

### What We DON'T DO:
- ❌ Collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers
- ❌ Track your browsing history
- ❌ Share your data with third parties
- ❌ Use analytics or telemetry
- ❌ Store text you read outside your browser

## Data Storage

All data (API keys, preferences) is stored **locally on your device** using Chrome's built-in storage API. This data:
- Never leaves your device except when making TTS API requests
- Can be cleared by uninstalling the extension
- Is not accessible to other extensions or websites

## Third-Party Services

This extension uses **Sarvam AI's Text-to-Speech API**:
- Selected text is sent to Sarvam AI for speech synthesis
- Sarvam AI's privacy policy applies to this data: https://www.sarvam.ai/privacy-policy
- You must have your own Sarvam AI API key

## Permissions Explained

- **storage**: Save your preferences and API key locally
- **activeTab**: Read selected text from the current webpage
- **contextMenus**: Add "Read with Vani: Indic TTS" to right-click menu
- **offscreen**: Play audio (Chrome's audio playback requirement)
- **scripting**: Inject content scripts for text highlighting

## Your Rights

You can:
- Clear all stored data by uninstalling the extension
- Review what's stored by opening Chrome DevTools → Application → Storage → Local Storage
- Stop using the extension at any time
